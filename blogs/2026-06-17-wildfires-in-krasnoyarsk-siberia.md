The VIIRS instrument aboard NOAA-20 captured false-color imagery of fires near the Yenisei River in Krasnoyarsk, Siberia on June 17, 2026, with active fire fronts marked by bright red and burn scars in dark red. The burned area grew from roughly 16,700 hectares on June 13 to 62,600 hectares by the morning of June 17, with local authorities attributing the rapid spread to high temperatures and dead trees from severe Siberian silkworm infestations. The associated data center is the MODIS Adaptive Processing System SIPS.
